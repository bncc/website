[ me, 2026 ]

Hi! My name is Luca. Welcome to my homepage!

I'm an engineering manager by trade, which mostly means I write documents, ask questions, and try to keep the room calm when things are on fire. I've been doing this for about six years. Before that, I was a software engineer for about a decade. Before that, I was a teenager who spent too much time on Usenet. Some of that is clearly still visible here.

I made this page because I remembered how fun the web used to be. There is no call-to-action, no newsletter, nothing to buy. It's just a page about me. Make yourself at home.

~ Luca
